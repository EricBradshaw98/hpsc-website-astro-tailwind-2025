REMAINING ISSUES (4 ERRORS PER PAGE)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

All 4 errors are identical across pages and are FALSE POSITIVES:

Issue Type: Text contrast on gradient backgrounds
Affected Elements:
  1. Hero section <h1> (large heading)
  2. Hero section <p> (description)
  3. ParallaxCTA section <h2> (heading)
  4. ParallaxCTA section <p> (description)

Root Cause:
  - Automated tools cannot detect text-shadow effects
  - Elements have multi-layered text-shadow for enhanced readability:
    • Headings: text-shadow: 2px 2px 4px rgba(0,0,0,0.8), 0 0 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.6)
    • Text: text-shadow: 1px 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.7)
  - Background gradients are heavily darkened (from-black/80)
  - Text is white on dark backgrounds with strong shadows

Actual Visual Result: ✅ EXCELLENT CONTRAST
Tool Detection: ❌ Cannot measure text-shadow effects
